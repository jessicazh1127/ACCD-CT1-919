Pet Shelter

Black circle: Pets on the street

Red circle: Pet shelter staff

Blue circle: People who want a pet

Yellow circle: Pets in the shelter

When Pet shelter staffs (red circle) detect Pets on the street (black circle) near them, they will bring with to the shelter so the pets will turn into Pets in the Shelter(Yellow circle), then when there is a person who want a pet (Blue circle), there will a connection between them.
