When Thumb Touches Index

At first, I wanted to create an interaction based on making a fist and then opening the hand, but I didn’t know how to move forward technically. In Week 9’s class, Maxim explained using the distance between two fingertips as a way to detect whether they are touching, and it suddenly made everything clear for me! So I tried using the distance between the thumb and index finger—and it turned out to be much easier than I had imagined!
